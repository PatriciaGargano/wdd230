Site Name: Bahía Blanca Chamber of Commerce

The site name includes "Chamber" to clearly identify its purpose as a local business organization. Bahía Blanca is chosen as it represents the city where the chamber operates, promoting local business activities and economic development.

Site Purpose

1. Facilitate business networking and collaboration among local enterprises.

2. Provide resources and information for economic development and community growth in Bahía Blanca.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming events promote business-to-business networking in Bahía Blanca?
  • Where can I find contact information for the Bahía Blanca Chamber of Commerce's board of directors?
  • What are the latest economic trends and opportunities in Bahía Blanca?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Light Blue (#90caf9) - Used for headings and background accents.

Secondary Color: White (#ffffff) - Used for backgrounds and text contrast.

Typography

Primary Font: Arial, sans-serif - Used for all text elements including headings and paragraphs.
